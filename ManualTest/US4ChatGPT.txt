Manual Test Instruction for ChatGPT.java:

Preview = ChatGPT.java takes questions (in the form of strings) 
          to get answers through the OpenAI API and then generates 
          responses using the GPT-3.5 architecture.

1. Launch the application
2. Click New question button
3. Ask "What is the largest city in the United States?"
4. After the question is asked, it shows answer: 
   "The largest city in the United States is New York City, 
   which is home to over 8 million people." is shown on the main board
5. Ask "Where is Spain?"
6. After the question is asked, it shows answer: 
   "Spain is located in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula. 
    It is bordered by Portugal to the west, France to the north, 
    and Andorra and the Bay of Biscay to the northeast." is 
    shown on the main board. 
7. Ask "what is the time."
8. After the question is asked, it shows answer:
   "It is 4:39 PM." is shown on the main board.
